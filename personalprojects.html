Personal Projects

As well as enjoying design, I also have have written for a variety of online lifestyle and fashion titles.

Emerald Street is a free daily email from the makers of Stylist magazine. It has become a looked-for part of the day for the 90,000-plus women who subscribe. I acted as a regular food contributor to Emerald Street, writing promotional writeups on latest launches and food reviews.

NeverUnderdressed.com was a high-end, glossy fashion and beauty website for the fashion savvy. Unfortunatly the website has closed since I wrote this fashion article on the high-street enigma, Whistles.

Stylist.co.uk is the web arm of Stylist magazine, one of the most-respected women's magazines in the UK - daily content is curated generating in excess of 6.3 million page impressions per month. I was charged by the web editor to write fashion articles based on trends.

Knowing my love for travel and food, ShortList.com's editor send me to live like a Dane for 48 hours in Copenhagen. I ate and drank my way around the city while appreciating their inante sense of style and architecture.
