Long live the King: LionHeart Lager

The first and flagship brew of the lineup is LionHeart Lager which pays homage to the bravery and fearlessness shown by King Richard the first of England as he led his Crusaders valiantly into battle. Like the King himself, LionHeart is bold and uncompromising. A premium all malt craft lager with a deep golden colour and a smooth finish, the beer is crafted using premium imported Belgian malt and packed with Saaz hops. LionHeart can be found on tap and in 330ml bottle form at bars, restaurants and stores around Cape Town.
