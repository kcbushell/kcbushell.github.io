Long live the King: LionHeart Lager

LionHeart Lager is bold and uncompromising. A premium all malt craft lager with a deep golden colour and a smooth finish, the beer is crafted using premium imported Belgian malt and packed with Saaz hops. LionHeart can be found on tap and in 330ml bottle form at bars, restaurants and stores around Cape Town, and at the Neighbourgoods Market in Johannesburg.

Meet the intrepid brewer

Meet Cliff Fitzhenry. He's the guy on the right taking a deep swig of the good stuff. A young geologist, Cliff grew bored of staring at rocks all day and decided to take his passion for craft beer to the proverbial next level. And so it was born: Kings Craft Beer and its flagship brew, LionHeart Lager.
